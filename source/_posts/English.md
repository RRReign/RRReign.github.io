## Day2
What we saw just now might be just `the tip of the iceberg`

The bar is pretty close to my office `as the crow flies`

这酒吧离我的办公室只有一条直线距离,as the crow flies 意思为像乌鸦一样飞，乌鸦是直线飞的，也就是直线距离很近的意思
